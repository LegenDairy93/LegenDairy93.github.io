How I build

Reliability is a product feature.

The useful layer is not another score. It is a legible chain from behavior, to review, to approval, to release.

01

Capture behavior

Preserve the output, tool use, trace, violations, and execution context of every important run.

02

Compare paths

Make behavioral changes visible even when two systems happen to return the same final answer.

03

Approve explicitly

A passing run is not automatically production behavior. Promotion should record who approved what and why.

04

Gate intentionally

Separate useful review information from release-blocking policy so teams control their own risk boundary.

05

Preserve provenance

Connect every artifact to its PromptDiff version, configuration, commit, branch, and CI execution.

06

Build in public

Keep the contracts understandable, the contribution surface clear, and the limitations honest.
